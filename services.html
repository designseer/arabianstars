Onshore & Offshore tanks cleaning 01

How we do

01

Our specialized offshore crude oil tank cleaning services offered by "ASOP" Environmental Services

Our experience and expertise in this field have made us a sought-after partner for companies in the industrial and offshore sectors. The offshore crude oil tank cleaning process can be complex and demanding, requiring a high level of skill and experience to ensure it is executed effectively and safely. Our team has over 15 years of experience in this industry, and is well-equipped to meet the unique needs of each project.

Outline the key steps of our process:

  • Step 1 – Removing oil/waste from the tank: Before any cleaning operation begins, we remove all materials from the tank. We can also manage the removal and disposal of these materials, collaborating closely with our clients to ensure safe handling and disposal practices.

  • Step 2 – Ventilation: Safety is paramount in enclosed spaces. We guarantee proper ventilation to mitigate risks to our team members when they enter the tank. 

  • Step 3 – Safety Checks: We conduct a rigorous series of safety checks, including draining the tank, testing for flammable gases, verifying steam connections, inspecting signage, and more. Our commitment to safety extends to your onsite team members as well.

  • Step 4 – Enclosed Space Entry Checklist: We follow comprehensive checklists for enclosed space entry, covering every essential aspect of safety. We understand that safety is of the utmost importance in offshore tank cleaning.

Our experienced team goes beyond these steps to ensure a thorough and safe tank cleaning process. We have also invested significantly in state-of-the-art equipment and comprehensive staff training to offer a wide range of services. In case of emergencies such as spillages, our team is ready to provide swift response and support.

At "ASOP" Environmental Services, we take pride in our industry-leading services, and we are committed to delivering results that exceed your expectations. When you choose to work with us, you can have confidence that our experienced team will manage every aspect of the process with precision and care.

For more detailed information about our offshore crude oil tank cleaning services or to discuss your specific requirements, please do not hesitate to get in touch with us. We are here to answer any questions you may have and provide you with the solutions you need.

Sludge & Slop disposal operations 02

How we do

02

Expert De-slopping & Desludging Services for Cleaner and Efficient Operations

We understand the pivotal role that environmental responsibility and operational efficiency play in the maritime, oil, and gas industries. Slop and sludge are persistent byproducts of the oil tank cleaning process on ship vessels, posing significant challenges for companies operating within these industries. These waste streams, formed when crude oil is emulsified with water and chemicals, demand meticulous attention and adherence to environmental regulations. Additionally, maintaining a clean system on your vessel is crucial for both efficiency and the longevity of your equipment.

At ASOP, we have positioned ourselves as experts in the field of desludging and de-slopping services, with a dedicated team of over 15 years of hands-on experience in managing and treating these waste streams. We recognize the environmental responsibilities incumbent upon the maritime industry and are committed to providing services that meet the required standards. All our operations strictly adhere to the disposal regulations set forth by the MARPOL, ensuring that waste water and sludge are handled and disposed of at approved facilities.

Our approach to these services aligns closely with environmental protection policies and frequently results in financial gains for ship owners. Our comprehensive services encompass the removal, transportation, safe disposal, and even recycling of slop oil into usable products. Upon completion, ship owners will receive a safe disposal certificate, detailing the volume of oil recovered and the content of water and sludge, simplifying compliance and documentation.

As a testament to our commitment, we are proud to offer our sludge oil slop discharging services at all major and minor ports in the UAE, ensuring accessibility and convenience for our clients.We invite you to explore our services and discover how ASOP can assist in the efficient and responsible management of slop and sludge waste streams within your operations.
